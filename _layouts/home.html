Georgia Tech iOS Club

Building a community of developers.


We support our members through a strong curriculum spanning iOS development and design. After just one semester, our members go on to develop their own apps, as well as receive internships and jobs in the tech industry.

Intermediate & Advanced

iOS Lab

Visit iOS Lab on Thursdays at 5:30pm in Instructional Center 215. It’s a new initiative we’re starting for intermediate/advanced iOS developers to come in and work on their projects. This includes getting time to build your own apps, viewing presentations on advanced topics, participating in discussions, and getting development help from our tech leads.
